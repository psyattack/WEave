Three steps to a perfect desktop

Setup takes less than a minute

01 //

Launch WEave

Download and open the app. It automatically detects your Wallpaper Engine installation directory.

02 //

Find Content

Use the search inside WEave or simply paste the links\ID to the wallpaper pages from the Steam workshop in the Multi-Download window.

03 //

Click "Install"

WEave will download the wallpaper itself to the desired folder.
